About Me

Hello! I’m Hitomi, a passionate and dedicated hair and makeup artist with over 12 years of experience in the beauty industry. My journey in the world of beauty began with a love for art and a deep appreciation for the transformative power of makeup and hairstyling.

Based in Sapporo/Japan, I specialize in providing personalized beauty services that enhance your natural beauty and leave you feeling confident and radiant. Whether you’re preparing for a special event, a wedding, or a photoshoot, my goal is to create a look that complements your unique style and personality.

Whether you're planning your dream wedding or preparing for a special event, I offer comprehensive wedding services tailored to your unique style. From flawless bridal makeup to elegant hairstyling, I ensure every detail complements your vision and enhances your beauty.

In addition to wedding services, I also provide transport services for your convenience. My team and I ensure that you have a stress-free experience, allowing you to focus on enjoying your special day.

If you're looking for the perfect dress to complete your look, I offer rental dress services featuring a curated selection of elegant and stylish options. Whether you need a chic gown for a formal event or a sophisticated dress for a casual gathering, you'll find the perfect piece with us.

With a commitment to excellence and a dedication to your satisfaction, I am here to make your beauty dreams a reality. Contact me today to discuss how we can create a look that perfectly suits your needs.
